# 🌿 Peace Within Chaos

*A calm digital space to express your chaos through art and words.*

## 🧠 Idea Behind the Project

Art has always been a form of therapy. Many people express emotions through writing, drawing, or simply placing their thoughts somewhere safe.

With this idea in mind, I created **Peace Within Chaos** — a small digital space where people can explore and interact with their own emotions.

This project is designed to be a **safe and reflective environment** where users can express their thoughts, chaos, and feelings without pressure or judgment.

Instead of focusing on likes, numbers, or performance, the goal is to create a space where **expression matters more than perfection**.

## ✨ Features

* 🎨 **Chaos Wall** – a free drawing space to express emotions visually
* ✍️ **Writing Space** – write thoughts like a personal journal
* 🖼 **Upload Artwork** – share artwork that represents your feelings
* 🔒 **Public / Private Options** – control who can see your expression
* 🌈 **Creative Tools** – color palette, brush size, rainbow brush, sparkle brush
* 🌍 **Public Gallery** – explore artwork shared by others

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The design of this project uses **dreamy and nature-inspired backgrounds** such as forests, galaxies, and calm skies.

These environments symbolize:

* peace
* reflection
* creativity
* emotional freedom

The idea is that even within chaos, **there can still be peace**.
